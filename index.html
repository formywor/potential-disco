The Gomega Unblocker V17 history

Here’s a condensed look at how we got to **V17**. Dates are summarized by milestone; the highlights capture the spirit of each jump.

V1 → V3: The Spark

Early experiments focused on speed and simplicity—snappy navigation, lightweight UI, and a low-footprint approach suitable for modest hardware.

V4 → V7: Stability & Controls
  • Refactors to reduce crashes/timeouts under load.
  • Basic telemetry (opt-in) for debugging performance issues.
  • Cleaner UX and better error states.
V8 → V12: Hardening
  • Improved session handling and edge-case reconnects.
  • Smart fallbacks to keep things responsive.
  • Early accessibility tweaks and keyboard-first navigation.
V13 → V16: UX polish & footprint
  • Neon-glass design system with dark backgrounds (the look you know).
  • Lower CPU overhead; fewer visual jank frames.
  • Better update checks and clearer version messaging.
V17: The Final Iteration
  • Faster startup, quieter errors, and a more consistent UI.
  • Smoother transitions and responsive layout across devices.
  • This release became the capstone before retirement.
